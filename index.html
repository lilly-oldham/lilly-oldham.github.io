About Me

My name is Lilly Oldham and I am a Life Sciences Illustration student at the Cleveland Institute of Art! As a student in the LSI department, I prefer to focus on drawing from life and observation, using all kinds of techniques and mediums. In addition to having a passion for drawing, I also enjoy photography and have an intense appreciation for all typse of art, from music and dancing to writing and more! I hope you enjoy looking through my portfolio and learning more about my work!
